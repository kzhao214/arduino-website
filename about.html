About Us

Arduino for Kids is a nonprofit STEM tutoring club affiliated with CAST-SD. Our program is designed to provide young students the opportunity to develop an interest in STEM, particularly in computer and electrical engineering, with introductory courses in Arduino.

Our Team

We are middle school and high school students enthusiastic about engineering and programming.
